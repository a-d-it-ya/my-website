The Log

A life in entries

2002
Origin
Born in West Bengal
Grew up curious. The kind of kid who asked why things worked instead of just accepting that they did. That never went away.
2018
School
Higher Secondary — 85%
Science stream. First real exposure to physics as something felt, not just memorized. Started reading Feynman around here. Something clicked.
2020
University
B.Tech ECE — Academy of Technology
Electronics and Communication Engineering. CGPA 8.63. The degree taught me circuits and signals — but the real education was building things that weren't in any syllabus.
2022
First real job
Flight Computer Lead — Aishyam Aerospace
Sole firmware owner for an aerospace-grade flight computer on an early-stage startup. Embedded C, Raspberry Pi Linux, deterministic control loops, fault-safe state machines. Boot sequencing to sensor drivers — all of it, alone.
This is where I learned what it means to be the last line of defense between code and gravity.
2023
Projects
Water tank monitor, farming system, gesture CV
Bare-metal embedded C. Low-power solar systems. Interrupt-driven architectures. Computer vision with OpenCV bridging into microcontroller UART. Learning by making things that actually run in the world.
2024
Graduated
Degree complete. Now what?
Graduated. Started Project-IRIS — a local AI system running Mistral 7B via Ollama on an RTX 3050. YOLOv8 for vision. ESP32-CAM with pan-tilt servos. Started calling it FRIDAY. Started thinking about what it could grow into.
NOW
Present
In the middle of everything
Preparing for GATE CSE. Building Project-IRIS. Exploring STM32 Nucleo + Zigbee mesh + Edge AI. Targeting embedded roles at companies like Qualcomm. And somewhere underneath all of it — thinking about Atlas Corporation every single day.
The work is not finished. It never will be. That's fine.
